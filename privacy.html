Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Pet Knowledge Auto Publisher ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

1. API Credentials

  • TikTok Client Key and Client Secret
  • TikTok Access Token and Refresh Token
  • TikTok Open ID

Purpose: To authenticate and publish content to your TikTok account via official TikTok API.

Storage: Stored securely in environment variables on your server. Never transmitted to third parties.

2. Video Metadata

  • Video titles, descriptions, and tags
  • Publishing timestamps
  • Video URLs

Purpose: To properly format and publish content to TikTok.

Storage: Stored locally in log files. Automatically deleted after 30 days.

3. Publishing Logs

  • Success/failure status of publications
  • Error messages (if any)
  • API response data

Purpose: For troubleshooting and service improvement.

Storage: Stored locally in log files. Automatically deleted after 30 days.

Information We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Financial information
  • Browsing history or cookies
  • Location data
  • TikTok follower data or analytics
  • Any information beyond what's necessary for content publishing

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  1. Authenticate with TikTok API
  2. Publish your content to your TikTok account
  3. Log publishing results for troubleshooting

We do NOT:

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track your TikTok account activity
  • Access your TikTok account beyond content posting

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure storage of API credentials in environment variables
  • No transmission of credentials over unencrypted connections
  • Access control to server and files
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

  • API Credentials: Stored until you manually delete them
  • Video Metadata: Retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted
  • Publishing Logs: Retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke API access at any time
  • Update your API credentials

To exercise these rights, simply:

  • Delete API credentials from your .env file
  • Revoke access in your TikTok Developer Portal
  • Delete log files from your server
